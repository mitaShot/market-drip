Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs) have revolutionized investing by making diversification cheap and easy.

## What is an ETF?

An ETF is a basket of securities that trades on an exchange like a stock. You can buy the S&P 500 in a single trade.

## Active vs. Passive

Most beginners should stick to passive index ETFs with low expense ratios.
