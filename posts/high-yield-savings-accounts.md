High-yield savings accounts (HYSAs) have been the go-to for emergency funds. But with rates shifting, are they still the best option?

## Current Rate Environment

While rates have dipped slightly from their peak, HYSAs still offer significantly better returns than traditional savings accounts.

## Alternatives

CDs and Money Market Accounts are viable alternatives, but HYSAs offer the best liquidity for emergency cash.
