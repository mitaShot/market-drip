Cash is king for flexibility, but points can offer outsized value for luxury travel.

## The Case for Cash Back

If you don't travel internationally, a simple 2% cash back card is hard to beat math-wise.

## The Case for Points

Redeeming points for business class flights can yield 4-5 cents per point in value.
